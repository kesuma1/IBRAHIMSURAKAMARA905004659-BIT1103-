About Us

The Sierra Leone Football Association (SLFA) is the governing body of football in Sierra Leone. We are committed to promoting the sport across all regions and nurturing talent at every level.

Our History

Founded in 1960, SLFA has played a pivotal role in shaping the football landscape of Sierra Leone. From grassroots development to national team achievements, our journey reflects resilience and passion for the game.

We have hosted major tournaments, built partnerships with global football bodies, and continue to invest in local clubs and academies.
